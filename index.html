ABOUT

Flash SAT is a web originally created for a computer science class, in Genuine school. Although its beginning seems a little forced, the idea of flash cards helping in the SAT where an idea the creator had planned to develop, although it was not originally thought to be a web-page, the technology class opened new possibilities for the idea.

HELP FLASH SAT

Although Flash SAT is a great tool, as you might already notice, the range of possibilities in the game itself is limited, as the development team was conformed by solidly one person, uploading and analysing ideas for the cards in each section was a slow process, and more when there were just two hands working on it. This whole “help” section has the purpose to invite all those people willing to make flash SAT a better site, whether you know html and css or just want to help.
